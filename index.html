A minimalist black screen clock for students and focus blocks

Designed for deep work, this zero-glare full-screen clock keeps your display active during long study sessions, Pomodoro timers, or exam prep without causing eye strain. The pitch-black background saves OLED battery life while processing native hardware wake commands to prevent your device from automatically locking.

Keep secondary monitors awake when idle

Operating multiple displays often results in aggressive OS idle timeouts. Leaving this web utility running on a secondary monitor keeps the screen awake natively without requiring administrative privileges or third-party background software. Ideal for dashboard monitoring, passive data viewing, or preventing forced idle logouts on corporate laptops.
